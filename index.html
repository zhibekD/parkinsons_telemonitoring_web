Parkinson's Telemonitoring Data Visualization Dashboard

Interactive visualizations of Parkinson's disease telemonitoring data

View 1: Patient Voice Variability Analysis

Explore voice variability metrics (Jitter and Shimmer) over time for individual patients. Interactive bar chart for patient selection and time series plots with LOESS smoothing.

View 2: Recording Frequency vs UPDRS Scores

Analyze the relationship between number of recordings per patient and their UPDRS scores. Includes scatter plot with regression line and linked violin plots showing score distributions.

View 3: Daily Patient Summaries

Examine daily aggregated metrics across patients, including UPDRS scores, DFA values, and test times. Interactive scatter plots with brushing for detailed exploration.
